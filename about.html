About Si Si Si Radio

Si Si Si Radio is a weekly curated listening experience featuring seven carefully selected tracks that explore different musical themes, genres, and eras.

Each Wednesday, we publish a new selection designed to expand your musical horizons and provide a thoughtfully sequenced listening journey.

Our mission is to celebrate music as an art form through mindful curation and contextual presentation, highlighting connections between artists, eras, and influences.

The Curator

Si Si Si Radio is curated by Silly Billy, a lifelong music enthusiast with a passion for discovering and sharing unique sounds from around the world. The project began in 2025 as a way to share musical discoveries and create coherent listening experiences in an era of algorithmic playlists.
